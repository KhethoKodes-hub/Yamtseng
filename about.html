The Yamtseng Story

A tale of vision, determination, and community transformation

The Beginning

In 2020, amidst global uncertainty, Moremi Wilfred Kgoro saw opportunity where others saw challenge. As a local entrepreneur from Postmasburg, he recognized the untapped potential within his community and founded Yamtseng SA (PTY) LTD with a clear mission: to uplift and empower through meaningful work and sustainable solutions.

The Foundation

With roots in construction and civil works dating back to 2016, our founder brought years of industry experience and deep community connections. This foundation became the bedrock upon which Yamtseng was built - not just as a business, but as a community partner committed to lasting change.

Community First

From day one, Yamtseng has operated on a simple yet powerful principle: community first. Every project, every service, every decision is made through the lens of how it can best serve and uplift the people of Postmasburg and the broader Northern Cape region.

Growth & Impact

Today, Yamtseng stands as a testament to what's possible when vision meets action. As a 100% black-owned enterprise, we've expanded our services across 13+ core areas while maintaining our commitment to employment equity and community development.

Our Journey

Key milestones that shaped our growth

2016

Foundation Years

Our founder begins journey in construction and civil works, building expertise and community relationships that would later become the foundation of Yamtseng SA.

2020

Yamtseng SA Born

Official establishment of Yamtseng SA (PTY) LTD (Reg: 2020/626106) with a clear mission to alleviate unemployment, hunger, and poverty through community-focused services.

2021

Service Expansion

Diversification into multiple service areas including catering, cleaning, procurement, and IT services to better serve community needs and create more employment opportunities.

Today

Community Leader

Established as a trusted partner across 13+ service areas, committed to 60% women employment and 2% disabled employment in permanent personnel, driving meaningful change in the Northern Cape.

Our Vision

"Transforming communities, one project at a time"

Our aim is to alleviate unemployment, hunger, and poverty through our work. We want to create employment opportunities that will eradicate crime by providing short-term and long-term jobs through various projects for local communities.

We will contribute to the economic growth of local municipalities, district municipalities, and the province through our impactful projects.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide everything we do

Respect

Yamtseng SA is an inclusive organisation where people of all creeds are welcomed and respected on their merits. We celebrate diversity and believe it makes us stronger.

Responsibility

We strive to act with integrity towards our staff, customers, the community, and the environment. Every action we take considers its impact on all stakeholders.

Caring

We maintain a genuine duty of care for our staff, customers, and the environment. This caring approach is reflected in everything from safety protocols to customer service.

Excellence

We seek to provide the best quality experience with regards to our services and customer relationships. Excellence is not a destination but a continuous journey.

Integrity

We maintain the highest standards of accountability, honesty, and openness in all we do. Our word is our bond, and we stand by our commitments without compromise.
